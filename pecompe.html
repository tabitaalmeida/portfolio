Pé com Pé

A children's footwear company, with 35 years of tradition and the market in Brazil and abroad, now with the virtual store project. In this project I was responsible for the entire store, since the visual part, as solutions to increase sales.

This project was a renewal option in the current layout of the store, which was approved by the client but has not been implemented yet. I used the colors of the palette, as well as the components and mascot.

I tried to bring a bit of joy and delicacy that is the children's universe, using the ripples at the top and at the bottom of the design. This work was thinking for be easy to use and purchase.
